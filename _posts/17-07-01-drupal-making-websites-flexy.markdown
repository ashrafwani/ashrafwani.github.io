<b>Dynamic Drupal websites with CMS, blogs, forums, e-commerce, multimedia and collaboration features – in a matter of hours.</b>

Drupal is software that allows an individual or a community of users to easily publish, manage, and organize a great variety of content on a website.

Drupal’s strength lies in its versatility, or, as we happen to mention a few times below... its flexibility.

<h2>Flexible Input</h2>
Enter content any way you like - define your own structured data types to control exactly what goes into your site without the need to re-engineer.

<h2>Flexible Management</h2>
Organise your site as you need it. Easily tag and categorise your content - without restrictive hierarchies.

<h2>Flexible Output</h2>
Dynamically re-mix your content for display. Powerful filters allow you to arrange your data on the fly.

<h2>Flexible Structure</h2>
Easily add, remove or enhance features – a clean modular framework enables your Drupal site to evolve as your requirements change over time.

<h2>Flexible Code</h2>
Enhance your Drupal site with custom features in no time at all.

Drupal 8 uses a unique “hook” system and an object orientated framework to give developers versatility with none of the overhead.

* OK, so it will take some time, but, honest, creating your own modules is amazingly easy, and I encourage you to try it!
